Scale

General

Cells

One cell is 8 x 8 meters.

The area of one cell is 64 m².

Zoning

The zoning grid is 4 cells deep.

Tiles

One tile is 240 x 240 cells or 1920 x 1920 meters.

The area of one tile is 57,600 cells, 3,686,400 m², 3.6864 km².

Map

The map is 9 x 9 tiles, 2160 x 2160 cells, 17280 x 17280 meters.

The area of the map is 81 tiles, 4,665,600 cells, 298,598,400 m², 298.5984 km².

Roads

Curbs

Sunken roads are 30 cm deep, therefore the curbs are 30 cm high.

Basic Road

The basic road is 2 cells or 16 meters wide.

The asphalt width is 10 meters.

The vehicle lanes are 3 meters wide, and the parking lanes are 2 meters wide.

Four-Lane Road

The four-lane road is 4 cells or 32 meters wide.

The asphalt width (including the median) is 22.4 meters.

The asphalt width on each side is 8 meters.

The median is 6.4 meters.

The vehicle lanes are 3 meters wide, and the parking lanes are 2 meters wide.

Highway Ramp

The ramp is 12 meters wide.

The asphalt width is 8 meters.

The lane width is 4 meters.

Highway (3 Lane)

The highway is 22 meters wide.

The asphalt width is 18 meters.

The lane width is 4 meters.

Tracks

Train

The train track gauge is 1.524 meters.

The distance between the centers of rails is 1.724 meters.

The rails are 40 cm wide at the base and 20 cm wide at the top.

The distance between the center of tracks in one direction to the other is 4 meters.

Tram

Tram tracks are the same as train tracks but they are sunken into the ground, and the two directions are closer together with a distance of 3 meters.

Monorail

Each monorail track is 66 cm wide.

The distance between the tracks is 3 meters.
